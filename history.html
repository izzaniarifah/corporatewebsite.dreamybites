2019

As the coronavirus pandemic spreads around the globe, it is causing widespread dread, worry, and concern
among the general public. Thus, Dreamy bites are inspired to offer delicious food and service for people
as a way to comfort them. In 2019, Dreamy Bites start a business with a small roadside stall.


2020

The idea to make Dreamy Bites run officially as a company and produce bakery products is created by
the four friends, in February 2020.


2021

The four friends officially made partnership agreements and registered Dreamy Bites Sdn Bhd under
the Company Commission of Malaysia (CCM) in May 2021. Meanwhile, on 16 June the operation of
the business is beginning in Petaling Jaya, Selangor.


2022

By 2022, Dreamy Bites made their bakery products available in the local markets and sought
more products in the bakery for the target market.


FUTURE PLAN

In future plans, the Dreamy Bites will begin to open more new branches in Malaysia and afterward start
to expand their business in Asia countries starting with Brunei.
